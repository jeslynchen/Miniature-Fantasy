HISTORY OF RE-MENT


RE-MENT Co., Ltd. (株式会社リーメント Kabushikigaisha-Riiment), located in Chiyoda Tokyo, is a Japanese manufacturer of collectible plastic toys.The company's name is derived from a combination of the phrase "reform the entertainment ", alluding to their desire for innovation in the toy market.

Established in 1998, Re-Ment currently sells a line of highly detailed miniature food, furniture and animal figures as well as mobile phone charms, doll fashions and magnets.

In 2008, the company began a collaborative partnership with Disney on a popular line of miniature toys featuring Mickey Mouse and characters from Toy Story, Winnie-the-Pooh, Alice in Wonderland and other stories. In July 2009, Re-ment partnered with Sanrio on a line of Hello Kitty miniatures. Re-Ment's most recent collaboration is with San-X on a line of Rilakkuma miniatures.
